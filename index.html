      Creative Codex

        New Goal

        Codex
        ◈

        A tool, not an app.

        Codex is built for the way a creative mind actually works — in captures, in sparks, in long slow goals. Not in notifications and streaks.

        ▣

        Tasks that age.

        Add tasks by weight — Light, Solid, or Heavy. Tasks you ignore slowly fade over 4 days. The app mirrors your real priorities, not your intentions.

        ⬡

        Field Notes.

        The fastest capture layer. A photo, a few words, an audio clip. The spark of an idea before you lose it. Syncs directly to your Obsidian vault.

        ◎

        Creative Codex.

        Long-term goals live here, separate from daily noise. Break them into steps and push those steps into your task list when you're ready to act.

        —

        No clutter. No gamification.

        Swipe left to delete. Hold to reveal options. The app gets out of your way.
